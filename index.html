About me

I’m a Master’s student in Human Centered Design & Engineering at the University of Washington, specializing in User Experience Research and Human-Robot Interaction (HRI). My passion is to bridge research, design, and engineering to create data-informed, user-centered solutions that enhance usability, trust, and engagement.

I focus on how design decisions and ethical frameworks shape human interaction with intelligent systems, and how insight-driven research can guide scalable, responsible innovation in AI and robotics. My goal is to build products and experiences that are not only efficient—but also emotionally meaningful and socially responsible.
